Current Projects

I am currently part of the Wearable Technology VIP team at Tandon where we are looking at ways to monitor anxiety levels using noninvasive biomarkers. I have created a Processing sketch to visualize current values received by the sensors. I also helped to build an infrared sensor which detects how much light is reflected back by the blood in a person's wrist. This value reflects when a heartbeat is sensed and can be used to determine blood glucose levels.
